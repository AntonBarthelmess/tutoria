About

Tutoria is a video tutorial sharing platform. Our aim is to enable people to learn new things on the web through high quality video tutorials. The tutorials are created by users for users. In order to make the learning easier, quicker and more convenient the tutorials follow the same pattern and come with an interactive guide.
